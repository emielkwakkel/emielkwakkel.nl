On a mission..

Human beings are becoming robots, processing complex user interfaces, scattered data sources on multiple devices. Technology brings advantages and at the same time it's making natural interactions less common.

My future world is one were technology adds meaning and is integrated in a way we'll forget it's there. Taking for example Amazon Alexa, allowing humans to talk to perform tasks, instead of using a smartphone or laptop. With my work I aim at making people lives richer, breaking down the technology wall keeping us from interacting.
