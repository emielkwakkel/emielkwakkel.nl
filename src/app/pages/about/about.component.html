Who is that guy?

Good question! Emiel is the Frontend Lead of Sogeti Netherlands. He won the title Sogetist of the Year in 2020. As Frontend Lead Emiel is responsible for the frontend expertise within Sogeti, together with the Sogeti Frontend Core Team. He is organiser of over 20 Frontend Meetups.

Bio

From a young age Emiel was already fascinated by the possibilities of the digitally connected world. He studied at the University of Applied Science of Arnhem and Nijmegen Communication and Multimedia Design. After four years of working for TradePoint as a frontend developer Emiel joined Sogeti. He worked for International Card Services, Rabobank and his current assignment is at the Dutch Police.
