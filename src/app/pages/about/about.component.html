Who is that guy?

Bio?

From a young age he was already fascinated by the possibilities of the digitally connected world. Emiel studied at the University of Applied Science of Arnhem and Nijmegen the study Communication and Multimedia Design, focussing on front-end, usability and interaction design. After his study Emiel worked as a front-end developer four years for TradePoint, working on brands like Curves Holland.
